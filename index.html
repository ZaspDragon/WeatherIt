Simple edge view
  • Use the current hourly forecast as the market expectation baseline.
  • Use the nearest station observation as the latest real-world reading.
  • A warmer actual than forecast can hint the day may be tracking hot; a cooler actual can hint the reverse.
  • Station observations can lag by about 20 minutes, so treat this as a directional tool, not gospel.
